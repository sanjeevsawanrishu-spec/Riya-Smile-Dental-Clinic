At Riya Smile Care Centre, she brings her expertise in teeth alignment, braces, and invisible aligners to help patients achieve beautifully straight smiles.

As a leading braces specialist in West Delhi, Dr. Anjali combines advanced orthodontic techniques with a patient-centered approach. Whether you're a teenager getting braces for the first time or an adult seeking discreet orthodontic options, Dr. Anjali provides compassionate care throughout your smile transformation journey.

Orthodontic Services by Dr. Anjali Aneja

Dr. Anjali provides comprehensive orthodontic solutions to correct misaligned teeth, bite problems, and jaw irregularities. Her services include:

Dental Braces

Traditional metal braces remain the most effective solution for complex orthodontic cases. Dr. Anjali uses high-quality brackets and wires to gradually move teeth into their ideal positions, correcting crowding, spacing, and bite issues.

Invisible Aligners

For patients seeking a discreet orthodontic option, Dr. Anjali offers invisible aligners - clear, removable trays that straighten teeth without the visibility of traditional braces. Ideal for adults and teens who want to maintain a natural appearance during treatment.

Early Orthodontic Intervention

Dr. Anjali provides interceptive orthodontics for children to address developing bite problems early. Early treatment can prevent more complex issues later and may reduce the need for extractions or surgery.

Bite Correction

Treatment for overbite, underbite, crossbite, and open bite problems. Proper bite alignment improves chewing function, reduces jaw pain, and enhances facial aesthetics.

Types of Braces Offered by Dr. Anjali

Dr. Anjali offers a variety of braces options to suit different patient needs, preferences, and budgets:

Metal Braces

Traditional stainless steel braces - most effective and affordable option for all types of corrections.

Ceramic Braces

Tooth-colored brackets that blend with natural teeth for a less noticeable appearance.

Self-Ligating Braces

Advanced braces with special clips that reduce friction and may shorten treatment time.

Clear Aligners

Nearly invisible removable trays for mild to moderate cases - the ultimate in discretion.
