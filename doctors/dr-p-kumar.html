About Dr. P. Kumar

Dr. P. Kumar is a dedicated and compassionate dentist practicing at Riya Smile Care Centre in Om Vihar, Uttam Nagar, Delhi. With a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S) degree and extensive clinical experience, Dr. Kumar has established himself as a trusted name in general and restorative dentistry across West Delhi.

Dr. Kumar's passion for dentistry is rooted in his commitment to helping patients achieve optimal oral health while maintaining the natural aesthetics of their smile. His gentle demeanor and patient-first approach have earned him the trust and appreciation of hundreds of families in Uttam Nagar and surrounding areas. Whether you're visiting for a routine dental check-up or require complex restorative work, Dr. Kumar ensures every patient receives personalized care in a comfortable and stress-free environment.

At Riya Smile Care Centre, Dr. Kumar works alongside a team of experienced dental professionals, utilizing advanced digital dentistry equipment including CAD/CAM technology, intra-oral scanners, and digital X-rays to deliver precise diagnoses and exceptional treatment outcomes for patients of all ages.

Qualifications & Experience

Dr. P. Kumar brings a strong academic foundation combined with hands-on clinical expertise to his practice at our dental clinic in Uttam Nagar:

  • B.D.S (Bachelor of Dental Surgery): Graduated from a recognized dental institution with comprehensive training in all aspects of general dentistry.
  • Clinical Experience: Years of experience treating patients across diverse dental needs, from preventive care to complex restorations.
  • Continuing Education: Regularly attends dental conferences, workshops, and training programs to stay updated with the latest advancements in restorative dentistry techniques.
  • Specialized Training: Advanced proficiency in aesthetic restorations, tooth-colored fillings, and prosthetic dentistry including crowns, bridges, and dentures.
  • In-House Lab Collaboration: Works closely with our on-site dental lab technicians to ensure custom prosthetics meet the highest standards of fit, function, and aesthetics.

Services Offered by Dr. P. Kumar

As a general and restorative dentistry specialist, Dr. Kumar provides a comprehensive range of dental treatments at our Om Vihar clinic to address various oral health concerns:

Dental Fillings

Dr. Kumar expertly treats cavities and tooth decay using modern composite (tooth-colored) fillings that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth. Using advanced laser filling techniques, the procedure is virtually painless and preserves maximum tooth structure while restoring strength and function.

Dental Crowns

For teeth that are severely damaged, weakened, or aesthetically compromised, Dr. Kumar designs and places custom dental crowns. We offer various crown materials including zirconia, metal-ceramic, and all-ceramic options to match your needs and budget. Our in-house lab ensures faster turnaround times.

Dental Bridges

Missing one or more teeth? Dr. Kumar provides fixed dental bridges that literally "bridge" the gap left by missing teeth. These prosthetics are anchored to neighboring teeth and restore your ability to eat, speak, and smile with confidence.

Complete & Partial Dentures

For patients with multiple missing teeth or complete tooth loss, Dr. Kumar crafts comfortable, natural-looking dentures. Whether you need full dentures or partial dentures, each prosthesis is custom-made in our dental lab for optimal fit and aesthetics.

Dr. Kumar's Approach to Patient Care

What sets Dr. P. Kumar apart is his patient-centered philosophy that prioritizes comfort, communication, and quality outcomes:

  • Thorough Diagnosis: Dr. Kumar believes in understanding the complete picture. He takes time to conduct comprehensive examinations using digital X-rays and intra-oral cameras before recommending any treatment.
  • Clear Communication: Every treatment option is explained in simple, easy-to-understand language. Dr. Kumar ensures patients are fully informed about their condition, available treatments, costs, and expected outcomes.
  • Gentle Techniques: Understanding that many patients experience dental anxiety, Dr. Kumar employs gentle techniques and a calm, reassuring manner to make every visit as comfortable as possible.
  • Affordable Care: True to Riya Smile Care Centre's charitable mission, Dr. Kumar is committed to providing quality dental care at affordable prices, with flexible payment options to accommodate all budgets.
  • Long-Term Oral Health: Beyond treating immediate concerns, Dr. Kumar educates patients on preventive care practices to maintain healthy teeth and gums for life.
