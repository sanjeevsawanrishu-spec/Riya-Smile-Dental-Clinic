Dr. S.K. Singh - Director, Riya Smile Care Centre

B.D.S | General Dentistry Specialist | 10+ Years Experience

About Dr. S.K. Singh

Director | B.D.S

Dr. S.K. Singh is the founding Director of Riya Smile Care Centre, a charitable dental clinic located in Om Vihar, Uttam Nagar. With over 10 years of dedicated experience in general dentistry, Dr. Singh has established himself as one of the most trusted dentists in West Delhi, serving thousands of patients from Uttam Nagar, Janakpuri, Dwarka, Nawada, and surrounding areas.

10+ Years Experience General Dentistry CAD/CAM Expert

Qualifications & Experience

Dr. S.K. Singh holds a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S) degree and has accumulated extensive hands-on experience treating a wide range of dental conditions. His commitment to continuous learning has led him to master the latest advancements in dental technology, particularly CAD/CAM digital dentistry.

  • Degree: Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S)
  • Experience: Over 10 years of clinical practice in general dentistry
  • Specialization: General Dentistry with expertise in restorative and preventive dental care
  • Technology Proficiency: CAD/CAM digital dentistry, intra-oral scanners, and digital X-ray systems
  • Leadership: Founder and Director of Riya Smile Care Centre since 2016

Under Dr. Singh's leadership, Riya Smile Care Centre has become a premier dental clinic in Uttam Nagar, equipped with an in-house dental laboratory that ensures faster turnaround times for crowns, bridges, and other dental prosthetics. His vision of providing quality dental care at affordable prices has helped countless families in West Delhi maintain optimal oral health.

Services Offered by Dr. S.K. Singh

As a general dentistry specialist, Dr. Singh provides comprehensive dental care for patients of all ages at the Om Vihar dental clinic. His services include:

  • Dental Implants: Permanent tooth replacement solutions using premium quality titanium implants
  • Root Canal Treatment: Painless RCT procedures using modern endo motors and apex locators
  • Crown & Bridges: Custom-crafted restorations using CAD/CAM technology for perfect fit
  • Teeth Whitening: Professional in-office whitening treatments for a brighter smile
  • Dental Fillings: Laser composite fillings that match natural tooth color
  • Scaling & Cleaning: Deep cleaning procedures to remove plaque and prevent gum disease
  • Tooth Extraction: Safe and painless tooth removal when necessary
  • Dentures: Complete and partial dentures crafted in the in-house dental lab
  • Preventive Dentistry: Regular check-ups, oral health education, and preventive treatments

Dr. Singh's Approach to Dental Care

Dr. S.K. Singh believes that quality dental care should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their economic background. This philosophy is the foundation of Riya Smile Care Centre's charitable mission. His patient-centric approach includes:

  • Painless Procedures: Using advanced anesthesia techniques and gentle handling to ensure comfortable treatments
  • Clear Communication: Taking time to explain diagnoses, treatment options, and expected outcomes in simple terms
  • Affordable Pricing: Offering competitive rates and flexible payment options for all dental procedures
  • Modern Technology: Leveraging CAD/CAM digital dentistry for precise, efficient treatments with faster results
  • Community Service: Conducting free dental check-ups every Sunday for underprivileged patients

Dr. Singh's dedication to combining traditional patient care values with cutting-edge dental technology has earned him the trust of thousands of families in Uttam Nagar and across West Delhi.
