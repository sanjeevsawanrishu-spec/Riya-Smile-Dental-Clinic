About Riya Smile Care Centre

Your trusted charitable dental clinic in Uttam Nagar since 2016

Our Story

Riya Smile Care Centre was founded in 2016 with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide quality dental care that everyone can afford. Located in the heart of Uttam Nagar, West Delhi, our clinic has grown to become a trusted name in community dental healthcare.

What started as a small practice has now evolved into a modern dental facility equipped with the latest technology, including CAD/CAM digital dentistry, intra-oral scanners, and RVG digital X-rays. Despite our technological advancements, we remain committed to our founding principle – making dental care accessible to all.

As a charitable dental clinic, we believe that financial constraints should never prevent anyone from receiving proper dental treatment. This is why we offer free dental check-ups every Sunday and maintain affordable pricing for all our services.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, high-quality dental care to the community of Uttam Nagar and surrounding areas in West Delhi. We strive to:

  • Provide affordable dental treatments without compromising on quality
  • Educate patients about oral health and preventive care
  • Serve the underprivileged community through free Sunday check-ups
  • Stay updated with the latest dental technologies and techniques
  • Create a comfortable, anxiety-free dental experience for all patients

Our Vision

To be recognized as the most trusted and accessible dental care provider in West Delhi, where every smile receives the care it deserves. We envision a community where quality dental healthcare is not a privilege but a right for everyone.

Why We're Different

Charitable Focus

We're not just a business – we're a community service. Our free Sunday check-ups have helped thousands of patients who couldn't afford dental care.

Modern Technology

From CAD/CAM systems to digital X-rays, we invest in technology that ensures accurate diagnosis and comfortable treatment.

Expert Team

Four qualified dentists with specialized training ensure you receive expert care for every dental need, from routine cleanings to complex procedures.

In-House Lab

Our on-site dental lab means faster turnaround for crowns, dentures, and other prosthetics, saving you time and multiple visits.
