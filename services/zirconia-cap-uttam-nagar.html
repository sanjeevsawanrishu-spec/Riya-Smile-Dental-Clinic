Premium metal-free dental crowns with natural appearance, crafted using advanced CAD/CAM technology in our in-house lab

What is Zirconia Crown?

A Zirconia crown is a premium dental restoration made from zirconium oxide, an exceptionally strong ceramic material. Unlike traditional metal-based crowns, Zirconia crowns are 100% metal-free and biocompatible, making them the ideal choice for patients seeking natural-looking, durable dental restorations.

At Riya Smile Care Centre in Om Vihar, Uttam Nagar, we use state-of-the-art CAD/CAM technology (Computer-Aided Design and Computer-Aided Manufacturing) in our in-house dental lab to create precisely-fitted Zirconia crowns. This advanced technology captures digital impressions of your teeth and mills your custom crown with exceptional accuracy—ensuring a perfect fit and natural appearance for patients across West Delhi.

Zirconia crowns are known for their remarkable translucency that mimics natural tooth enamel, making them virtually indistinguishable from your real teeth. Whether you need to restore a damaged front tooth or protect a weakened molar, our expert dentists can help you achieve a beautiful, functional smile.

Benefits of Zirconia Crowns

Choosing Zirconia crowns at our Uttam Nagar clinic offers numerous advantages over traditional crown materials:

  • Natural Appearance: Zirconia's translucent properties perfectly mimic natural tooth enamel, creating a seamless blend with surrounding teeth. Your crown will be virtually undetectable.
  • Exceptional Strength: Zirconia is one of the strongest dental materials available, capable of withstanding heavy biting forces—ideal for both front and back teeth.
  • 100% Metal-Free & Biocompatible: No risk of allergic reactions or metal sensitivities. Safe for patients with nickel or metal allergies.
  • No Dark Gum Lines: Unlike PFM crowns, Zirconia doesn't create unsightly dark lines at the gum margin, even as gums naturally recede over time.
  • Temperature Resistant: Zirconia doesn't conduct heat or cold like metal, reducing tooth sensitivity to temperature changes.
  • Long-Lasting Durability: With proper care, Zirconia crowns can last 15-20 years or longer—outlasting most other crown materials.
  • Stain Resistant: The smooth ceramic surface resists staining from coffee, tea, and other colored foods and beverages.
  • Minimal Tooth Reduction: Modern Zirconia crowns require less tooth structure removal compared to some traditional crowns.

Our Zirconia Crown Procedure

At Riya Smile Care Centre, we follow a precise, technology-driven process to deliver superior Zirconia crowns:

  1. Consultation & Examination: Our dentist thoroughly examines your tooth, takes digital X-rays, and discusses whether a Zirconia crown is the best option for your specific case.
  2. Tooth Preparation: The affected tooth is carefully reshaped under local anesthesia. We remove minimal tooth structure while ensuring adequate space for the crown.
  3. Digital Scanning (CAD): Instead of messy traditional impressions, we use our advanced CAD/CAM scanner to capture precise 3D digital images of your prepared tooth and surrounding teeth.
  4. Crown Design (CAM): Using specialized software, your custom Zirconia crown is digitally designed for optimal fit, function, and aesthetics.
  5. In-House Milling: Your crown is milled from a solid block of high-quality Zirconia in our on-site dental lab—eliminating delays from external laboratories.
  6. Shade Matching & Finishing: We carefully match the crown's shade to your natural teeth and apply finishing touches for a natural look.
  7. Final Fitting & Cementation: The completed crown is checked for fit, bite, and appearance, then permanently bonded to your tooth.

Thanks to our in-house CAD/CAM lab, patients from Uttam Nagar and nearby areas can get their Zirconia crowns in just 2-3 visits over 5-7 days—significantly faster than clinics relying on external labs.
