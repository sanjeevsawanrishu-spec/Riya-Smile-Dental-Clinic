What Are Dental Crowns?

A dental crown, also known as a tooth cap, is a custom-made restoration that completely covers a damaged, decayed, or weakened tooth. Crowns restore the tooth's shape, size, strength, and appearance while protecting it from further damage. They are permanently cemented onto the existing tooth structure or dental implant.

At Riya Smile Care Centre in Om Vihar, Uttam Nagar, we use advanced CAD/CAM technology in our in-house dental lab to create precise, perfectly-fitting crowns. This means faster turnaround times and exceptional quality for patients across West Delhi—often completing treatment in just 2-3 visits.

What Are Dental Bridges?

A dental bridge is a fixed prosthetic device used to replace one or more missing teeth. It consists of artificial teeth (pontics) that are anchored to crowns placed on the adjacent natural teeth or implants. Bridges literally "bridge" the gap created by missing teeth, restoring your smile and bite function.

Unlike removable dentures, dental bridges are permanently fixed in place, providing stability and comfort. They prevent remaining teeth from shifting, maintain facial structure, and allow you to eat and speak normally. Types of Dental Crowns

We offer a comprehensive range of crown materials to suit different needs and budgets:

  • Zirconia Crowns: The premium choice for natural aesthetics and durability. Made from solid ceramic zirconium oxide, these crowns offer exceptional strength, a translucent natural appearance, and are 100% metal-free and biocompatible. Ideal for front teeth and those with metal allergies.
  • PFM Crowns (Porcelain-Fused-to-Metal): A reliable and affordable option combining metal's strength with porcelain's aesthetics. The metal base provides excellent durability while the porcelain coating matches your natural tooth color. Suitable for both front and back teeth.
  • Metal Crowns: Made from gold alloy or base metal alloys, these are the strongest and most durable option. They require minimal tooth removal and withstand heavy biting forces. Best suited for back molars where aesthetics are less of a concern.
  • All-Ceramic Crowns: Provide the best color match and are ideal for patients with metal allergies. They offer excellent aesthetics for front teeth restorations.

Benefits of Crown & Bridge Treatment

Choosing dental crowns and bridges at our Uttam Nagar clinic offers numerous advantages:

  • Restores Function: Eat your favorite foods and speak clearly without discomfort or difficulty.
  • Natural Appearance: Modern crowns and bridges look just like natural teeth, enhancing your smile.
  • Protects Weak Teeth: Crowns strengthen damaged, cracked, or root canal-treated teeth, preventing further damage.
  • Prevents Teeth Shifting: Bridges stop adjacent teeth from moving into the gap left by missing teeth.
  • Maintains Facial Structure: Prevents the sunken appearance that can occur with missing teeth.
  • Long-Lasting Solution: With proper care, crowns and bridges can last 10-20 years.
  • Easy Maintenance: Care for them like natural teeth with regular brushing and flossing.

Our Crown & Bridge Procedure

At Riya Smile Care Centre, we follow a systematic approach using advanced technology for optimal results:

  1. Consultation & Examination: Our dentist examines your teeth, takes X-rays, and discusses the best treatment options based on your needs and budget.
  2. Tooth Preparation: The tooth is reshaped by removing a thin layer of enamel to accommodate the crown. For bridges, the anchor teeth are prepared similarly.
  3. Digital Impressions: We use advanced CAD/CAM technology to capture precise digital impressions of your teeth, ensuring a perfect fit.
  4. Temporary Crown: A temporary crown protects your prepared tooth while your permanent restoration is fabricated in our in-house lab.
  5. Crown Fabrication: Our on-site dental lab crafts your custom crown or bridge within 3-5 days—faster than external labs.
  6. Final Fitting & Cementation: The permanent crown/bridge is checked for fit, bite, and color match, then permanently cemented.
