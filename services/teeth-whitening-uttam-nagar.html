What is Professional Teeth Whitening?

Professional teeth whitening is a cosmetic dental procedure that removes stains and discoloration from your teeth, giving you a brighter, more confident smile. At Riya Smile Care Centre in Uttam Nagar, we use advanced LED light-activated whitening technology that delivers superior results compared to over-the-counter products.

Unlike home whitening kits, our in-office treatment is performed by experienced dentists who customize the procedure based on your tooth sensitivity and desired shade. The whitening gel we use contains higher concentrations of active ingredients, ensuring faster and more dramatic results in just one visit to our Om Vihar clinic.

Our Whitening Procedure

At Riya Smile Care Centre in Uttam Nagar, our teeth whitening process follows a systematic approach for optimal results:

  1. Initial Consultation: Our dentist examines your teeth, discusses your goals, and determines if you're a suitable candidate for whitening.
  2. Dental Cleaning: We recommend professional scaling before whitening to remove plaque and tartar for better gel penetration.
  3. Shade Assessment: We record your current tooth shade to measure improvement after treatment.
  4. Gum Protection: A protective barrier is applied to shield your gums from the whitening gel.
  5. Gel Application: Professional-grade whitening gel is carefully applied to the front surface of your teeth.
  6. LED Activation: The gel is activated using our advanced LED light, accelerating the whitening process.
  7. Final Results: After 2-3 cycles of gel application, you'll see dramatically whiter teeth.

Most patients near Om Vihar and Uttam Nagar achieve 5-8 shades whiter teeth in a single appointment at our clinic.

Who Can Get Teeth Whitening?

Professional teeth whitening is suitable for most adults with healthy teeth and gums. Ideal candidates include:

  • Adults with yellowed or stained teeth from food, drinks, or aging
  • People with extrinsic stains from coffee, tea, wine, or tobacco
  • Individuals preparing for special occasions like weddings or interviews
  • Anyone seeking to enhance their smile and boost confidence

However, whitening may not be recommended for:

  • Pregnant or nursing women
  • Children under 16 years of age
  • Patients with severe tooth decay or gum disease (treatment first)
  • Those with intrinsic stains from tetracycline or fluorosis
  • People with crowns, veneers, or fillings on front teeth (these won't whiten)

Our dentists at Riya Smile Care will assess your oral health during consultation and recommend the best treatment for your specific needs.
