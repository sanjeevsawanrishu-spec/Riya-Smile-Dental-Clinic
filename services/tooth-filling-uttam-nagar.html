What is Dental Filling?

A dental filling is a restorative treatment used to repair teeth that have been damaged by decay (cavities) or minor fractures. When bacteria in your mouth produce acids that erode tooth enamel, it creates holes in the teeth called cavities. If left untreated, these cavities can grow larger and cause pain, infection, or even tooth loss.

At Riya Smile Care Centre in Uttam Nagar, Delhi, we provide high-quality dental fillings using modern materials and techniques. Our experienced dentists serve patients from Om Vihar, Bindapur, Dwarka Mor, Nawada, and all areas of West Delhi with affordable and effective cavity treatment.

The filling procedure involves removing the decayed portion of the tooth, cleaning the cavity, and filling it with a suitable material to restore the tooth's shape, strength, and function. Early treatment of cavities with fillings prevents the need for more extensive procedures like root canal treatment.

Types of Dental Fillings

We offer various types of filling materials to suit different needs, preferences, and budgets. Our dentists will recommend the best option based on the cavity location, size, and your specific requirements:

  • Composite (Tooth-Colored) Fillings: Made from a mixture of plastic resin and glass particles, composite fillings match your natural tooth color perfectly. They are ideal for visible front teeth and provide excellent aesthetics. These fillings bond directly to the tooth structure, providing additional support.
  • Amalgam (Silver) Fillings: A time-tested material made from a mixture of metals including silver, mercury, tin, and copper. Amalgam fillings are extremely durable, long-lasting (10-15 years), and cost-effective. They are best suited for back teeth (molars) where chewing pressure is highest.
  • Glass Ionomer Cement (GIC) Fillings: Made from acrylic and glass powders, GIC fillings release fluoride over time, helping to prevent further decay. They are commonly used for fillings below the gum line, for children's teeth, and for temporary restorations. While less durable than other options, they offer unique protective benefits.

Benefits of Tooth Filling

Getting a dental filling is one of the most common and beneficial dental treatments. Here's why you should not delay treating cavities:

  • Prevents Further Decay: Fillings seal off spaces where bacteria can enter, stopping decay from spreading deeper into the tooth
  • Restores Tooth Function: Regain the ability to chew and bite comfortably without pain or sensitivity
  • Preserves Natural Tooth: Save your natural tooth structure and avoid extraction
  • Quick & Painless: Most fillings can be completed in a single visit of 30-45 minutes with local anesthesia
  • Cost-Effective: Treating cavities early with fillings is much cheaper than root canal or extraction later
  • Natural Appearance: Tooth-colored composite fillings are virtually invisible, maintaining your smile's aesthetics
  • Long-Lasting Results: With proper care, fillings can last many years, protecting your tooth

Our Filling Procedure

At Riya Smile Care Centre in Uttam Nagar, we follow a careful, patient-friendly approach to ensure comfortable and effective treatment:

  1. Examination & Diagnosis: Thorough dental examination and digital X-ray to assess the cavity size and depth
  2. Local Anesthesia: Numbing the area around the affected tooth for a pain-free experience
  3. Decay Removal: Carefully removing all decayed and damaged tooth material using precision dental instruments
  4. Cavity Preparation: Cleaning and shaping the cavity to ensure proper filling adhesion
  5. Filling Application: Placing the chosen filling material in layers and shaping it to match your tooth
  6. Curing & Hardening: For composite fillings, using a special LED light to harden the material
  7. Bite Adjustment: Checking and adjusting your bite to ensure comfortable chewing
  8. Polishing: Smoothing and polishing the filling for a natural look and feel

Signs You Need a Filling

Early detection of cavities leads to simpler treatment. Visit our dental clinic near Metro Pillar 706 in Uttam Nagar if you experience any of these warning signs:

  • Tooth Sensitivity: Pain or discomfort when eating hot, cold, or sweet foods and drinks
  • Visible Holes: Dark spots or visible holes/pits on the surface of your teeth
  • Toothache: Mild to moderate pain in a tooth, especially when biting down
  • Food Trapping: Food frequently getting stuck between teeth or in a specific spot
  • Rough Edges: Feeling rough or sharp edges on a tooth with your tongue
  • Chipped Tooth: Minor chips or fractures that need restoration
  • Old Filling Damage: Existing filling that has cracked, worn, or fallen out

Don't wait for pain to worsen. Regular dental check-ups at Riya Smile Care help detect cavities early when they're easiest to treat.
