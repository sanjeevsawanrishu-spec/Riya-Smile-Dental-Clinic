What Are Invisible Aligners?

Invisible aligners are custom-made, transparent plastic trays that fit snugly over your teeth to gradually shift them into proper alignment. Unlike traditional metal braces, these clear aligners are virtually invisible when worn, making them an ideal choice for adults and teens who want to straighten their teeth without anyone noticing.

Each set of aligners is precisely engineered using 3D digital scanning technology to apply controlled pressure on specific teeth. You'll wear each aligner set for 1-2 weeks before moving to the next set in the series, progressively moving your teeth toward their final position. The entire treatment typically involves 15-30 sets of aligners, depending on your case complexity.

Benefits of Clear Aligners

Invisible aligners offer numerous advantages over traditional braces, making them increasingly popular among our patients in Uttam Nagar and West Delhi:

  • Nearly Invisible: Clear plastic material makes aligners virtually undetectable, allowing you to smile confidently during treatment
  • Removable: Take them out for eating, drinking, brushing, and special occasions - no food restrictions
  • Comfortable: Smooth plastic edges without metal wires or brackets mean no mouth irritation
  • Better Oral Hygiene: Remove aligners to brush and floss normally, reducing risk of cavities and gum problems
  • Fewer Visits: Typically requires fewer dental visits compared to traditional braces adjustments
  • Predictable Results: 3D treatment planning shows your expected results before treatment begins
  • No Emergency Visits: No broken brackets or poking wires that require urgent appointments

Our Aligner Treatment Process

At Riya Smile Care Centre, we follow a comprehensive treatment process to ensure optimal results with your invisible aligners:

  1. Initial Consultation: Dr. Anjali Aneja examines your teeth, discusses your goals, and determines if clear aligners are suitable for your case
  2. Digital Scanning: We take precise 3D digital impressions of your teeth using advanced intraoral scanners - no messy molds needed
  3. Treatment Planning: Using specialized software, we create a customized treatment plan showing the step-by-step movement of your teeth and expected final results
  4. Aligner Fabrication: Your custom aligners are manufactured using high-quality, medical-grade clear plastic material
  5. Aligner Delivery: You receive your first sets of aligners along with detailed wearing instructions and care guidelines
  6. Progress Check-ups: Regular visits every 4-6 weeks to monitor progress and receive new aligner sets
  7. Retention Phase: After treatment, you'll wear retainers to maintain your new smile permanently
