Types of Braces We Offer

At our dental clinic in Uttam Nagar, we offer multiple braces options to suit your needs, lifestyle, and budget:

Metal Braces

Traditional metal braces are the most common and affordable option for orthodontic treatment. Made from high-quality stainless steel, these braces are highly effective for correcting even complex alignment issues. Modern metal braces are smaller and more comfortable than ever before.

Ceramic Braces

Ceramic braces work like metal braces but use tooth-colored or clear brackets that blend with your natural teeth. They're an excellent choice for adults and teens who want a more discreet orthodontic treatment while still getting effective results.

Self-Ligating Braces

Self-ligating braces use a special clip instead of elastic bands to hold the wire in place. This results in less friction, fewer adjustments, shorter appointments, and often a faster treatment time. They're available in both metal and ceramic options.

Benefits of Orthodontic Treatment

Braces do more than just straighten your teeth. Here are the key benefits of orthodontic treatment:

  • Improved Smile Aesthetics: Straight, properly aligned teeth enhance your facial appearance and boost confidence
  • Better Oral Health: Aligned teeth are easier to clean, reducing the risk of cavities and gum disease
  • Corrected Bite Problems: Fix overbite, underbite, crossbite, and open bite issues that can cause jaw pain
  • Improved Speech: Proper teeth alignment can help resolve speech difficulties caused by misaligned teeth
  • Reduced Wear: Even distribution of biting pressure prevents excessive wear on certain teeth
  • Long-term Investment: A healthy, beautiful smile lasts a lifetime with proper retainer use

Our Braces Procedure

At Riya Smile Care Centre, we follow a systematic approach to ensure the best orthodontic outcomes:

  1. Initial Consultation: Dr. Anjali Aneja examines your teeth, takes X-rays and impressions, and discusses your treatment goals
  2. Treatment Planning: A customized treatment plan is created based on your specific needs, with a detailed timeline and cost estimate
  3. Braces Placement: Brackets are carefully bonded to each tooth and connected with archwires. The process is painless and takes about 1-2 hours
  4. Regular Adjustments: You'll visit every 4-6 weeks for wire adjustments to gradually move your teeth into position
  5. Braces Removal: Once treatment is complete, braces are removed and retainers are provided to maintain your new smile

Who Needs Braces?

Orthodontic treatment can benefit people of all ages. You may need braces if you have:

  • Crooked or misaligned teeth
  • Overcrowded teeth with insufficient space in the jaw
  • Gaps or spaces between teeth
  • Overbite (upper teeth extend too far over lower teeth)
  • Underbite (lower teeth extend past upper teeth)
  • Crossbite (some upper teeth sit inside lower teeth)
  • Difficulty chewing or biting food properly
  • Jaw pain or TMJ issues related to bite problems

Both children and adults can benefit from braces. The ideal age for evaluation is around 7-10 years, but it's never too late for orthodontic treatment. Many of our patients in Uttam Nagar and West Delhi are adults seeking to improve their smile.
