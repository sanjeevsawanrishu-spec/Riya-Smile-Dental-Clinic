Custom-made dentures crafted in our in-house dental lab for a natural, comfortable fit

What Are Dentures?

Dentures are removable dental prosthetics designed to replace missing teeth and restore your smile. Whether you've lost teeth due to decay, gum disease, or injury, dentures offer an affordable and effective solution to regain your ability to eat, speak, and smile with confidence.

At Riya Smile Care Centre in Om Vihar, Uttam Nagar, we specialize in creating custom-fitted dentures that look natural and feel comfortable. Our in-house dental laboratory allows us to craft high-quality dentures with precise fit, faster turnaround times, and competitive prices for patients across West Delhi.

Types of Dentures We Offer

We provide various denture options to suit your specific needs and budget at our Uttam Nagar clinic:

Complete Dentures (Full Dentures)

Complete dentures replace all teeth in either the upper or lower jaw, or both. They rest directly on the gums and are ideal for patients who have lost all their natural teeth. Our complete dentures are designed to provide a natural appearance and comfortable fit.

Partial Dentures

Partial dentures are used when you still have some healthy natural teeth remaining. They fill the gaps left by missing teeth and attach to your existing teeth using metal clasps or precision attachments. This prevents your remaining teeth from shifting and maintains proper alignment.

Flexible Dentures

Flexible dentures are made from a lightweight, bendable material that offers superior comfort compared to traditional rigid dentures. They have no metal clasps, making them more aesthetically pleasing. Flexible dentures adapt to the natural movements of your mouth, providing a secure and comfortable fit.

Benefits of Dentures

Choosing dentures at our Uttam Nagar dental clinic provides numerous advantages:

  • Restored Smile: Dentures fill gaps and give you a complete, natural-looking smile that boosts your confidence.
  • Improved Eating: Enjoy your favorite foods again with properly fitted dentures that restore chewing function.
  • Clear Speech: Missing teeth can affect pronunciation; dentures help you speak clearly and confidently.
  • Facial Support: Dentures provide support to facial muscles, preventing the sunken appearance caused by tooth loss.
  • Affordable Solution: Compared to dental implants, dentures offer a cost-effective way to replace multiple missing teeth.
  • Non-Invasive: No surgery required; dentures are a simple, removable solution that's easy to maintain.
  • Custom Fit: Each denture is crafted specifically for your mouth, ensuring optimal comfort and appearance.

Our Denture Process

At Riya Smile Care Centre, we follow a systematic approach to create perfectly fitted dentures:

  1. Initial Consultation: Our dentists examine your oral health, discuss your needs, and recommend the best denture type for your situation.
  2. Precise Impressions: We take detailed impressions of your gums and any remaining teeth to ensure accurate denture fabrication.
  3. In-House Fabrication: Your dentures are crafted in our on-site dental lab, ensuring quality control and faster delivery.
  4. Trial Fitting: You try the dentures to check fit, bite alignment, and appearance. Adjustments are made as needed.
  5. Final Delivery: Once perfect, your custom dentures are fitted, and we provide care instructions for long-lasting use.
  6. Follow-Up Care: We schedule regular check-ups to ensure your dentures remain comfortable and make adjustments when needed.
