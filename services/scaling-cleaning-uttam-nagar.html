Scaling & Teeth Cleaning in Uttam Nagar

Professional dental cleaning to remove plaque, tartar, and stains for healthier teeth and gums

What is Dental Scaling?

Dental scaling is a professional cleaning procedure that removes plaque, tartar (calculus), and stains from your teeth surfaces. At Riya Smile Care Centre in Uttam Nagar, we use advanced ultrasonic scalers that gently vibrate to break down hardened deposits without damaging your tooth enamel.

Even with regular brushing and flossing, plaque can accumulate in hard-to-reach areas and harden into tartar within 24-72 hours. Once tartar forms, it cannot be removed by normal brushing and requires professional dental cleaning. Our experienced dentists in Om Vihar provide thorough scaling to eliminate these harmful deposits and prevent gum disease.

Regular dental scaling is essential for maintaining optimal oral health. It helps prevent cavities, gingivitis, and periodontitis while keeping your breath fresh and your smile bright.

Professional teeth cleaning at our West Delhi clinic offers numerous advantages for your oral and overall health:

Our Scaling Procedure

At Riya Smile Care Centre, our teeth cleaning process is thorough, comfortable, and typically completed in 30-45 minutes:

  1. Oral Examination: Our dentist examines your teeth and gums to assess the extent of plaque and tartar buildup and check for any underlying issues.
  2. Ultrasonic Scaling: Using an advanced ultrasonic scaler, we gently remove tartar deposits from all tooth surfaces, including areas between teeth and along the gum line.
  3. Hand Scaling: For stubborn deposits, our dentist uses manual scaling instruments to ensure thorough cleaning in difficult-to-reach areas.
  4. Polishing: A special polishing paste is applied to remove surface stains and smooth tooth surfaces, making it harder for plaque to accumulate.
  5. Fluoride Treatment: Optional fluoride application strengthens enamel and provides added protection against decay.
  6. Oral Hygiene Guidance: We provide personalized tips for maintaining oral health between visits.

For patients with gum disease or deep pockets, we may recommend deep cleaning (root planing) to clean below the gum line and promote healing.

How Often Should You Get Scaling?

The frequency of professional teeth cleaning depends on your individual oral health needs:

  • Every 6 Months: Recommended for most adults with healthy gums and good oral hygiene habits
  • Every 3-4 Months: Ideal for patients with history of gum disease, diabetes, or heavy tartar buildup
  • Every 3 Months: Recommended for smokers, pregnant women, and those with periodontal disease

Signs that indicate you need immediate scaling include:

  • Bleeding gums while brushing or flossing
  • Persistent bad breath despite regular brushing
  • Visible yellow or brown deposits on teeth
  • Red, swollen, or tender gums
  • Teeth appearing longer due to gum recession

Our dentists at Riya Smile Care will assess your oral health and recommend the optimal cleaning schedule for you.
