Save your natural tooth with advanced, pain-free RCT at Riya Smile Care Centre

What is Root Canal Treatment?

Root canal treatment (RCT), also known as endodontic therapy, is a dental procedure designed to save a severely infected or damaged tooth. When the soft tissue inside your tooth (called the pulp) becomes infected due to deep decay, cracks, or trauma, it can cause severe pain and lead to abscess formation if left untreated.

At Riya Smile Care Centre in Uttam Nagar, Delhi, our experienced dentists perform painless root canal treatments using state-of-the-art technology. We serve patients from Om Vihar, Bindapur, Dwarka Mor, Nawada, and all areas of West Delhi with affordable, high-quality endodontic care.

During the procedure, the infected pulp is carefully removed, the root canals are cleaned and shaped, and then filled with a biocompatible material. This eliminates infection and saves your natural tooth from extraction.

Benefits of Modern RCT

Modern root canal treatment has evolved significantly with advanced technology, making it a comfortable and highly successful procedure. Here's why you should choose RCT over tooth extraction:

  • Painless Procedure: With modern endo motors, rotary files, and effective anesthesia, RCT is virtually pain-free
  • Single-Sitting RCT: Advanced equipment allows us to complete most treatments in just one visit
  • Preserves Natural Tooth: Keep your original tooth, which is always better than artificial replacements
  • High Success Rate: Modern RCT has a success rate of over 95% with proper care
  • Prevents Spread of Infection: Eliminates bacteria and prevents the infection from spreading to other teeth or jaw
  • Cost-Effective: More affordable than extraction followed by implant or bridge
  • Natural Appearance: With a crown, your treated tooth looks and functions like natural teeth

Our RCT Procedure

At Riya Smile Care Centre, we follow a systematic approach to ensure the best outcomes for our patients in Uttam Nagar and West Delhi:

  1. Diagnosis & X-Ray: Digital RVG X-ray to assess the extent of infection and root canal anatomy
  2. Local Anesthesia: Effective numbing to ensure you feel no pain during the procedure
  3. Access Opening: Creating a small opening in the tooth crown to access the pulp chamber
  4. Pulp Removal: Using rotary endo motors to carefully remove the infected pulp tissue
  5. Canal Shaping: Cleaning and shaping the root canals with precision using apex locators for accurate measurement
  6. Irrigation: Thorough cleaning with antimicrobial solutions to eliminate all bacteria
  7. Filling: Sealing the canals with gutta-percha, a biocompatible rubber-like material
  8. Crown Placement: Fitting a dental crown to protect and restore the tooth's function

Who Needs Root Canal Treatment?

You may require root canal treatment if you experience any of the following symptoms. Visit our dental clinic near Metro Pillar 706 in Uttam Nagar for proper diagnosis:

  • Severe Toothache: Persistent, throbbing pain especially when chewing or applying pressure
  • Prolonged Sensitivity: Pain that lingers after exposure to hot or cold foods/drinks
  • Tooth Discoloration: Darkening or graying of the tooth indicating pulp death
  • Swollen Gums: Tenderness, swelling, or a pimple-like bump on the gums near the tooth
  • Cracked or Chipped Tooth: Trauma that has exposed the pulp to bacteria
  • Deep Cavity: Decay that has reached the pulp chamber
  • Previous Dental Work: Repeated procedures on the same tooth

Don't ignore these warning signs. Early treatment can save your tooth and prevent complications.
