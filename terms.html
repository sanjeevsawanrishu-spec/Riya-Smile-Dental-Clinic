Description of Services

Riya Smile Care Centre is a charitable dental clinic located in Uttam Nagar, Delhi, providing various dental treatments including but not limited to:

  • Root canal treatment
  • Orthodontic treatment (braces and aligners)

  • Crowns, bridges, and dentures

  • Tooth extraction and fillings
