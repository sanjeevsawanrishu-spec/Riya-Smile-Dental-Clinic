How to Reach Us

Riya Smile Care Centre is conveniently located in Om Vihar Phase 1, Uttam Nagar West, making it easily accessible from all parts of West Delhi.

By Metro

The nearest metro station is Uttam Nagar West on the Blue Line. From the metro station, our clinic is just a 5-minute walk towards Metro Pillar 706.

By Auto/Taxi

Simply tell the driver to take you to "Metro Pillar 706, Om Vihar Phase 1, near Reform Gym". Our clinic is on the first floor.

Landmark

Look for Reform Gym near Metro Pillar 706. Our clinic is located on the first floor of the same building (WZ43).
